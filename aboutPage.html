Welcome to our webpage, traveller! 3 Swords Republic is a Bristol & London based party series and label, aiming to bring together a community of music and art lovers in a raucous showdown for the ages.

The 3 Swords origins story is one that has floated around the internet for millenia. It has worn many masks and been the silent guest at many dinner tables. Now I am here, at last, to set the record straight. The true tale begins at dusk, on a balmy evening in the south west a mystical far away land. But first, let us feast our ears!

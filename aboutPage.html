Welcome to our webpage, traveller! 3 Swords Republic is a Bristol & London based party series and label, aiming to bring together a community of music and art lovers in a raucous showdown for the ages.

The 3 Swords origins story is one that has floated around the internet for millenia. It has worn many masks and been the silent guest at many dinner tables. Now I am here, at last, to set the record straight. The true tale begins at dusk, on a balmy evening in the south west a mystical far away land. But first, let us feast our ears! Tell your navigator to set sail for the links below, and we shall dine together before the beast awakens and strips us of our freedoms!
